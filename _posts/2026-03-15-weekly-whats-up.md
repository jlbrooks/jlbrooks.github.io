## What I'm reading
I finished His Dark Materials and am halfway through The Secret Commonwealth, the second book in Philip Pullman's [The Book of Dust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Book_of_Dust) follow-up trilogy. The original books were a joy to re-read, and so far I am loving the followups. [La Belle Sauvage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/La_Belle_Sauvage) was probably my favorite book in the series so far - gripping and absolutely lovely characters.
