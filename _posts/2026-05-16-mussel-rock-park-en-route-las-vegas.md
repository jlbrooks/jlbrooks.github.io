Rory and I are in Las Vegas this weekend to visit my grandparents. Leaving a bit early for the airport, we made a brief stop at Mussel Rock Park on the coast.

![](assets/img/2026-05-16-2026-05-16-mussel-rock-park-en-route-las-vegas/IMG_4433.jpeg)

I thought it would just be a nice beach/cliff area, turns out it’s a favorite spot for paragliders!

![](assets/img/2026-05-16-2026-05-16-mussel-rock-park-en-route-las-vegas/IMG_4418.jpeg)

We wandered around for a bit, watching the ocean and the planes taking off from SFO overhead with one paraglider giving us a show close by.

![](assets/img/2026-05-16-2026-05-16-mussel-rock-park-en-route-las-vegas/IMG_4427.jpeg)

![](assets/img/2026-05-16-2026-05-16-mussel-rock-park-en-route-las-vegas/IMG_4431.jpeg)

What fun! Will definitely be back.
